Car Repair and Auto Body Shop Management Odoo Apps

Car and Vehicle Repair management Odoo apps provide complete solution for Automotive service workshop management for fleet-Vehicle. This Odoo app is useful for any kind of Garage Management,car service center, Vehicle Service Management System, auto repair shop for spareparts and Body Shop service industry to manage service repair process for cars or any fleets and Vehicle. This Odoo app includes All stages of car services starting from Repair Request ticket, Diagnosis, workshop job order assignment of technician or mechanics and supervisor for inspection checks which include Service types and spare parts required , even inventory of part are managed along with Accounting invoice which include billing and payment process in Odoo.

This Odoo Apps helps to manage repair order process and workflow of fleet and Vehicle to Auto Workshop and Auto Body Shop Management companies. After installing this odoo module user can also have option to register car repair request from the website and portal also from the Backend. After the car repair request submitted technician can start diagnosis problem of the fleet and able to fill out timesheet for the spent hours. Technical can able to create workorder for different repair process and service manager will create quotation based on required consumed parts and timesheet and complete flow of repair process. This car repair Odoo apps also support different pdf reports repair order report, label etc.
